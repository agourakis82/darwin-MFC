# GRADE EVIDENCE LEVELS

- **Ia**: Systematic review/meta-analysis of RCTs
- **Ib**: At least one RCT
- **IIa**: At least one well-designed controlled study without randomization
- **IIb**: At least one quasi-experimental study
- **III**: Descriptive studies (comparative, correlation, case-control)
- **IV**: Expert opinion, clinical experience

# CONVERGENCE STATUS

- **convergencia**: SUS and Medical Societies agree on indication, population, frequency
- **parcial**: Agree on indication but differ on population or frequency
- **divergencia**: Disagree on indication or methods
- **em_disputa**: Active scientific controversy with conflicting evidence
