## Anti-Personas

### Who We're NOT Building For

**The Hospital Specialist:**
- Focus: Inpatient/specialty care
- Needs: Different clinical tools
- Why excluded: Not primary care focused

**The Researcher Only:**
- Focus: Academic research
- Needs: Publication databases
- Why excluded: Not clinical practice

**The Tech-Averse Practitioner:**
- Actively resists digital tools
- Prefers paper-only workflow
- Why excluded: Cannot be served by digital platform
